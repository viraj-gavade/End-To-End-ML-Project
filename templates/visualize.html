Key Insights from the Data

Gender Impact

Female students tend to perform slightly better in reading and writing scores, while gender doesn't show a strong correlation with math performance.

Parental Education

Students whose parents have higher education levels (Bachelor's or Master's degrees) generally show better overall performance.

Test Preparation

Students who complete test preparation courses show significantly higher average scores across all subject areas.

Lunch Impact

Students with standard lunch programs tend to show better performance than those with free/reduced lunch programs, which may indicate socioeconomic factors.
